I’m sitting by my gray windowsill. On the gray windowsill, there are three green flower pots, over a dozen yellow leaves, a few gusts of wind playing with the wind chimes, a silent melody from Pagi! Two paintings and three letters.

Before and behind me is youth. Though I don’t know exactly how old “young” is.

Me-xừ Dan once had a rough idea about age: everyone is just two zeros. 2 0.

Everyone in life exists between two nothings. The past—the first nothing—is what no longer exists. The future—the second nothing—is the unknown. All humans walk, stand, lie, sit, float, run, crawl, creep… all sit idly between these two nothings. This is the present.

So, the present is two zeros. Everyone is two zeros. Neither old nor young. Everyone is the same. Equal.

Those who claim to be young rely on the second nothing: the future. Those who claim to be old rely on the first nothing: the past. Those who are neither old nor young are in the present: 2 0.

The act of naming a day, month, and year is a backward counting exercise because people assume life follows a linear upward progression.

So, Friday - day no.6 must certainly come after Thursday - day no.5 . But strangely, no one calls Sunday the Eighth day. Because how can the eighth day go back to being the second - Monday? Da Fu*k?

The universe doesn’t follow such a linear path. It spins around and around. Indeterminate, floating in the cosmic space.

If we live a full life, we should count backward. Like 2024 Day - 5 Month - 12 Year . Because in reality, life is a countdown to zero.

When you’re born, you’re given a number, a countdown, like waiting for the New Year. Counting down to the finish line, number zero. Death.

Only on the day you die will you know the number you were given. Everyone has a different number. Except for zero, everyone is the same. Death!

So, after all, naming days, months, and years is just mathematics.

But life, fundamentally, is still 2 0.

I put down my pen, ending the act of journaling. I return to life and the cyclical linear mathematics: 5th december, 2024.
