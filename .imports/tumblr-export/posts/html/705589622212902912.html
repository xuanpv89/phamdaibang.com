Floating

There is a man
Into the leafless garden
Pick a dried rose
Put it in the pocket
Then walk away

Beside a dried river
Picking up a boneless stone
Put it in to pocket
Go home

The river flows again
The place sprouts a seed
The forest seems to be revived

He put his pocket on a rock
The water letting the pocket
float away
